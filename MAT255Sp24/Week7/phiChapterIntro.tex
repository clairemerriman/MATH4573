Recall from Section \cref{sec:FlT-eulerFLT}, \nameref{defn:phi-fn}

\begin{defn}[Euler $\phi$-function]
    Let $n$ be a positive integer. The \emph{Euler $\phi$-function} $\phi(n)$ is \[\phi(n)=\#\{a\in\Z : a>0 \textnormal{ and } (a,m)=1\}.\]
\end{defn}
