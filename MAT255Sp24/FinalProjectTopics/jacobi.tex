  For part (e) in addition to the hints in Strayer, it may help to use that 
	\begin{align*}
		p^k&=\left(1+(p-1)\right)^k\\
		&=1+(p-1)+\frac{k!}{(k -2)! 2!}(p-1)^2
		+\frac{k!}{(k -3)! 3!}(p-1)^3+\cdots
		+\frac{k!}{(k -1)! 1!}(p-1)^{k-1}
		+(p-1)^k
	\end{align*}
